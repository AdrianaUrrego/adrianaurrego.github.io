Students who couldn't find appropriate content quickly disengaged, resulting in poor completion rates and low satisfaction with the English Academy.

03 — How It Works

Adaptive Assessment System

The test uses an adaptive algorithm with a curated question bank.

Assessment Flow

Test Introduction

Students self-select their perceived proficiency level to establish the starting point for adaptive questioning.

Multi-Competency Questions

Students answer questions testing grammar, speaking, listening, writing, and reading skills. The interface adapts to each question type.

AI Speech Evaluation

For speaking questions, advanced AI evaluates pronunciation in real-time, measuring fluency (natural flow), completeness (accuracy), and prosody (expressiveness).

Adaptive Difficulty

The algorithm continuously adjusts question difficulty based on performance. Correct answers advance students to harder questions, while incorrect answers provide easier ones.

Results & Recommendations

Students receive immediate CEFR level placement with a personalized learning path tailored to their specific level and skill gaps.
