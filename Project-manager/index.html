Project Manager

Fuel Control Systems projects

As a product manager in SCI, I had the opportunity to lead around 50 different projects to produce and install Fuel Control Systems in the Middle East, Latin America, and Central America. 90% of these projects were handled remotely, using PMP as a standard to accomplish the schedule and budget defined by the customer.
I worked with a wonderful team of installers, assemblers, shippers, and vendors who helped me to reach the customer's spectations in terms of quality and performance of the product.

FleetSAP

A Fuel Control Platform developed to track the data generated by the Fuel Control Systems installed, that provides real-time information to the customers about their fuel consumption, stock, and fleet location.
I worked closely with the development team, in order to turn requirements into features; our main goal was to give customers a tool to optimize their fuel consumption.

Logistic web and mobile applications

I was fortunate to work as project lead for the last development stage of the MVP for a mobile and web application in order to create, control, and track logistics services, such as Freight Forwarding, Long Haul, First Mile, and Last Mile.
Here I had the opportunity to lead more than 20 developers using the Agile methodology to fulfill the schedule planned. We created weekly sprints with small deliverables for each one of the platforms for 4 months until we finished the whole MVP. After that, we continued working with SCRUM to handle the continuous release of features.
