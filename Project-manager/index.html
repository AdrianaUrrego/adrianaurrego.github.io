Project Manager

Fuel control projects

As a product manager in SCI, I had the opportunity to lead around 50 different projects to produce and install Fuel Control Systems in the Middle East, Latin-American, and Central America, 90% of these projects were handled remotely, using PMP as a standard to accomplish the schedule and budget defined with the customer.
I worked with a wonderful team of installers, assemblers, shippers, and vendors who helped me to reach the customer's speciation about the quality and performance of the technology.

FleetSAP

A fuel control platform developed to track the data generated by the Fuel Control Systems installed to give real-time information to the customers about their Fuel consumption, stock, and fleet location.
I worked closely with the development team to create features required for the success of each one of the projects, our main goal was to give to our customers a tool to optimize their fuel consumption.

Logistic web and mobile applications

I was fortunate to work as project lead for the last stage development of the MVP for a mobile and web application to create, control, and track logistics services from Freight Forwarding to Long Haul, First Mile, and Last Mile.
Here I had the opportunity to lead more than 20 developers using the Agile methodology to fulfill the schedule planned. During 4 months, we create weekly sprints with small deliverables for each one of the platforms until we finished the whole MVP, after that, we continue working with SCRUM to handle the continuous release of features.
